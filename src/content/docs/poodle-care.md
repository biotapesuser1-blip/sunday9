Caring for a poodle involves understanding its unique grooming, health, and activity needs. Here are some tips:

### Grooming
Poodles require regular grooming to maintain their iconic curly coats. This includes brushing, bathing, and professional trimming.

### Nutrition
Provide a balanced diet tailored to your poodle's size and activity level. Avoid overfeeding to prevent obesity.

### Exercise
Ensure your poodle gets daily physical activity, as they are active and intelligent dogs. Regular mental stimulation is also essential.

### Health Care
Schedule routine veterinary visits, and stay alert for breed-specific conditions such as hip dysplasia or eye issues.

### Socialization
Poodles are social and thrive with companionship. Introduce them to various environments, people, and pets to keep them happy and well-adjusted.
