Welcome to the detailed FAQ page. Here, we cover less common questions and provide more in-depth answers.

### Can I adopt multiple pets at once?
Yes, you can adopt multiple pets as long as you meet requirements for space, care, and financial stability.

### What do I do if my pet gets sick?
Contact your veterinarian immediately. Always follow professional medical advice closely.

### Are special toys required for pets in specific environments?
Certain pets like aquatic animals or large birds need specialized toys to meet their needs. Check with pet supply stores for recommendations.
