## Taking Care of Your Hamster

Taking care of your hamster involves providing a suitable cage, nutritious food, fresh water, and regular cleaning. Here are some tips:

1. **Cage:** Ensure your hamster has enough space to move around. Opt for materials like glass or sturdy plastic to prevent escapes.
2. **Food:** Offer a balanced diet of hamster pellets, seeds, and occasional fresh fruits or vegetables. Avoid toxic foods like chocolate or onions.
3. **Water:** Provide fresh water daily through a water bottle to avoid contamination.
4. **Hygiene:** Clean the cage weekly and replace bedding regularly.
5. **Health:** Regularly check your hamster for signs of illness such as lethargy or changes in appetite.

Proper care will ensure your hamster stays happy and healthy!
